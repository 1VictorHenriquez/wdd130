Barquisimeto, Venezuela

Barquisimeto is a city on the Turbio River and is the capital of the state of Lara, in northwestern Venezuela. It is known for its modern cathedral and the Barquisimeto Museum, which exhibits regional works of art. The Bararida Zoological and Botanical Institute is a park and zoo with pumas, hippos and manatees.
